Muishi Cartoon Mallard Duck Swaddle Blanket & Hat Set

This adorable swaddle blanket and matching hat set is designed to wrap your baby in a soft, secure embrace that mimics the comforting feel of the womb. Perfect for newborns 0-6 months, it offers a cozy sleeping environment while keeping them warm and safe.

Constructed from gentle, skin-friendly high-quality polyester, this set ensures maximum comfort and reduces irritation for your baby's delicate skin. The unique cartoon mallard duck design adds a playful and cheerful element to daily routines, making it a delightful addition to nursery decor.
