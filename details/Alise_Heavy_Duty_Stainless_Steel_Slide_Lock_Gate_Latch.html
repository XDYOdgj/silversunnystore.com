Alise Sliding Bolt Latch

Crafted from durable SUS304 stainless steel, this Alise sliding gate latch delivers unmatched strength and corrosion resistance. It's engineered to thrive in both indoor and outdoor environments, withstanding extreme weather and mechanical stress without rusting or breaking. The thickened build ensures it's built to last, making it an excellent choice for heavy-duty doors, gates, and cabinets.

The central-positioning design adds flexibility, allowing for easy installation on either side of the door. Whether you're securing a gate, a front door, or a storage cabinet, this latch offers a reliable, user-friendly solution. Its 10-inch white barrel bolt features a solid 14mm bolt and an 8mm padlock hole, enhancing security and providing options for added protection.
